# Booking Service - User Guide

## 🎯 How to Book a Service

### Step 1: Browse Services
1. Log into your QuickServ dashboard
2. Scroll down to see "Popular Services"
3. Browse through available service categories:
   - 🔧 Plumbing
   - 💇 Salon & Beauty
   - 🚗 Auto Repair
   - 🧹 Cleaning
   - ⚡ Electrician
   - 🏠 Home Repair

### Step 2: Select a Service
1. Click on any service card
2. A modal will open showing all available sub-services
3. Review the options with:
   - Service name
   - Duration estimate
   - Price
4. Click "Book Now" on your desired service

### Step 3: Complete Booking Form

#### Service Details
- Your selected service will be displayed at the top
- Shows service icon, name, duration, and price

#### Choose Your Location

You have two options:

##### Option A: Use Current Location (GPS) 📍
1. Click the "Use Current Location" button
2. Your browser will ask for permission to access location
3. Click "Allow" in the browser prompt
4. Wait a few seconds while we detect your location
5. Your city and state will automatically fill in

**What you'll see:**
- Loading spinner with "Detecting location..." text
- Your location appears in the input field
- If there's an error, a message appears below the field

##### Option B: Enter Location Manually 📝
1. Click in the location input field
2. Start typing your city name
3. A dropdown will appear with matching cities
4. Click on your city from the list
5. The location field will be filled

**Available Cities:**
- Mumbai, Maharashtra
- Delhi, Delhi
- Bangalore, Karnataka
- Hyderabad, Telangana
- Chennai, Tamil Nadu
- Kolkata, West Bengal
- Pune, Maharashtra
- Ahmedabad, Gujarat

**Tips:**
- You can type any part of the city or state name
- The list filters as you type
- Click the X button to clear and start over

### Step 4: Confirm Booking
1. Review your service details and location
2. Click the "Confirm Booking" button
3. You'll see a success message
4. A provider will contact you shortly

## 📍 Location Selection Tips

### Using GPS Detection

**When to use:**
- You're at the location where service is needed
- You want quick, accurate location
- You're comfortable sharing your location

**What you need:**
- Location services enabled on your device
- Internet connection
- HTTPS connection (secure website)

**If GPS doesn't work:**
- Check if location services are enabled
- Make sure you clicked "Allow" in browser
- Try manual entry instead
- Check your internet connection

### Manual Entry

**When to use:**
- You don't want to share GPS location
- You're booking for a different location
- GPS detection failed
- You prefer typing

**How to search:**
- Type city name: "Mumbai"
- Type state name: "Maharashtra"
- Type both: "Mumbai Maharashtra"
- Partial matches work: "Mum" shows Mumbai

## ⚠️ Troubleshooting

### "Location permission denied"
**What it means:** You clicked "Block" or "Deny" in the browser prompt

**Solution:**
1. Use manual entry instead, OR
2. Reset browser permissions:
   - Click the lock icon in address bar
   - Find "Location" permission
   - Change to "Allow"
   - Refresh the page and try again

### "Location unavailable"
**What it means:** Your device can't determine location

**Solution:**
- Check if location services are enabled on your device
- Make sure you have internet connection
- Try manual entry instead

### "Location request timed out"
**What it means:** GPS took too long to respond

**Solution:**
- Try clicking "Use Current Location" again
- Make sure you have good GPS signal
- Use manual entry if problem persists

### "Please select or enter your location"
**What it means:** You tried to confirm without selecting a location

**Solution:**
- Click "Use Current Location" OR
- Type and select a city manually
- Make sure the location field is filled

### Location field is empty after GPS detection
**What it means:** GPS detection failed silently

**Solution:**
- Check the error message below the field
- Try again or use manual entry
- Ensure you have internet connection

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I have to use GPS?**
A: No, you can always enter your location manually.

**Q: Is my location saved?**
A: No, it's only used for this booking and then cleared.

**Q: Can I book for a different location?**
A: Yes, just type the location manually instead of using GPS.

**Q: What if my city isn't in the list?**
A: You can still type it in the input field.

**Q: Can I change location after selecting?**
A: Yes, click the X button and select again.

**Q: Why does GPS take time?**
A: GPS needs to communicate with satellites, usually takes 5-10 seconds.

**Q: Does it work offline?**
A: No, you need internet for GPS detection and booking.

**Q: Can I skip location?**
A: No, location is required to match you with nearby providers.
